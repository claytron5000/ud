Urology Directory

Urology is also know as genitourinary surgery. It is the branch of medicine that focuses on the unrinay track system and the male reproduction system. These include the following organs: kidneys, adrenal glands, ureters, urinary bladder, urethra, and the male reproductive organs (testes, epididymis, vas deferens, seminal vesicles, prostate, and penis).
